Why This Event Is Super Cool?

Raspberry Jams are independently organised community events where people get together to share knowledge, learn new things, and meet other Raspberry Pi enthusiasts. Attending a Jam is a great way to find out more about the Raspberry Pi and Open Hardware, learn what you can do with it, and meet like-minded people.

Event Schedule

  • Day 01 (March ,22nd)

9:30 AM - 10:00 AM

Registration & Welcome

10:00 AM - 10:15 AM

Welcome & Introduction

10:15 AM - 11:00 AM

Kickstarting Your Raspberry Pi Journey

Abhay P A

Are you curious about Raspberry Pi and how it can power your next project? This session is your gateway to the world of Raspberry Pi, where we'll cover everything from setup to real-world applications. Whether you're a student, maker, or tech enthusiast, you'll explore how to use this tiny computer for coding, IoT, and automation. Learn how to turn simple ideas into powerful solutions with hands-on tips and examples, and take your first steps into the world of digital making with confidence and creativity using Raspberry Pi.

11:00 AM - 11:45 AM

Simplifying IoT Communication: MQTT with Raspberry Pi

Ashish Joy

Ever wondered how smart devices talk to each other? This session introduces MQTT, the lightweight protocol perfect for Internet of Things (IoT) communication. Learn how to use Raspberry Pi as both an MQTT broker and client to send and receive data efficiently. Discover how this powerful protocol can drive your automation projects, remote sensing systems, or connected devices. Whether you're building a home setup or exploring industrial IoT, you'll gain hands-on experience in setting up your own MQTT-powered network using simple tools and real-world examples—all on a Raspberry Pi.

11:45 AM - 12:30 PM

Edge AI on Raspberry Pi 5 AI Kit

Alosh Denny

Curious about Edge AI and Raspberry Pi 5? This session introduces you to real-time AI at the edge using the Hailo AI accelerator and optimized machine learning models. Learn the basics of TinyML, and discover how to run powerful models like Deepseek, Llama, and Gemini efficiently on the Pi. Whether you're a beginner or an AI enthusiast, you'll see how computer vision and inference can work seamlessly on small hardware. Explore hands-on use cases, performance tips, and practical workflows to bring intelligent edge computing to life with Raspberry Pi 5 and the Hailo AI Kit.

12:30 PM – 01:15 PM

A Cloud of Your Own: Hosting with Raspberry Pi 5

Rishi Krishna S

What if you could host your own website or cloud server using Raspberry Pi 5? This session shows how to build a self-hosted, secure, and efficient system for managing your data and services. You'll learn how to run a private cloud, host web apps, and take control of your digital life without relying on third-party platforms. Ideal for students, developers, or anyone concerned with data privacy. By the end of this talk, you'll be equipped to set up and manage your own cloud infrastructure using free, open-source tools—all powered by a Pi on your desk.

02:00 PM – 02:45 PM

Take Control of Your Internet: Pi-hole on Raspberry Pi 5

Shaan Shoukath

Tired of intrusive ads and trackers on every device? In this session, you'll learn how to use Pi-hole with Raspberry Pi 5 to build a network-wide ad blocker. Discover how to install and configure Pi-hole, filter out unwanted content, and enjoy a faster, cleaner, and more private internet experience. Whether you're tech-savvy or new to the Pi world, this session gives you the tools to control your digital space. Say goodbye to annoying pop-ups and hello to smoother browsing for your whole home or office—all with a simple and powerful Pi-based solution.

02:45 PM - 03:30 PM

Build Your Own Digital Signage System with Anthias

Salman Faris

Learn how to transform any screen into a smart display using Anthias, an open-source digital signage platform made for Raspberry Pi. In this session, you'll go through the steps to install, configure, and manage content like dashboards, announcements, or media. Whether it's for schools, offices, or events, Anthias offers a cost-effective and customizable solution. You'll explore the full setup, get practical tips for deployment, and see how to keep your signage up-to-date with minimal effort. Perfect for makers and organizers who want to power displays without paying for expensive commercial systems.
